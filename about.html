Press Release
Who We Are
We are the Syndicate of Lebanese owners and operators of guesthouses. Also known as
DIYAFA
Mission & Main Objectives

To promote collaboration, excellence, and sustainability within the guesthouse industry, while advocating for the interests of our members and fostering a culture of innovation and hospitality

To launch a website that will gather all the Lebanese Guesthouses under one portal and provide them with individual pages

To create a general page that will be dedicated to the syndicate for all the general and administrative affairs, publications, exchanges with the members

Membership benefits

Advocacy and representation: Representing the collective interests of guesthouse owners in regulatory matters, industry standards, and policy-making to create a favorable operating environment.

Knowledge sharing and networking: Facilitating the exchange of best practices, information, and resources among members to enhance their operational efficiency, service quality, and overall business performance.

Training and development: Providing educational programs, workshops, and training sessions to improve the skills and knowledge of guesthouse owners and their staff, promoting professional growth and career advancement.

Sustainability and responsible tourism: Promoting sustainable practices, environmental stewardship, and responsible tourism initiatives within the guesthouse industry to minimize the ecological footprint and contribute to the local community's well-being.

Marketing and promotion: Collaborating on marketing campaigns, partnerships, and promotional activities to increase the visibility and competitiveness of member guesthouses, attracting more guests and driving business growth.

⁠Industry standards and quality assurance: Establishing and upholding industry standards for guesthouse operations, service quality, cleanliness, and safety to ensure a consistent and exceptional experience for guests across all member establishments.

Research and innovation: Encouraging research, innovation, and the adoption of new technologies within the guesthouse sector to enhance customer experience, operational efficiency, and business sustainability.

By focusing on these objectives, the syndicate can support the growth and success of guesthouse businesses while contributing to the overall development and professionalism of the industry.

Join Our Syndicate

Click on “JOIN US” button, fill the questionnaire prepared by DIYAFA and submit it, we will reach out out to you after finish internal review

Once you have filled the questionnaire, we will process your information and validate it, a page for your guesthouse will be created on the DIYAFA WEBSITE PORTAL

Don’t forget to enclose photos of the premises, interior and exterior, photos of the host (s) with their names and any other specific visual material you would like to include on your page. A main page photo representative of your guesthouse to be exhibited under the guesthouse name.
